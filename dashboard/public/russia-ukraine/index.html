核心判断框架 Core Analytical Framework

到2026年6月，俄乌战争已凝固成三重消耗的稳态：战场上，无人机透明化封锁了双方机动，没有一方能取得战役级突破；财政上，两国都吃紧，但脆弱性性质根本不同——俄罗斯是内生、慢性、自控的失血，乌克兰是外生、可能骤断、命在他人的悬崖；政治上，俄罗斯是"压制式稳定"，乌克兰是"动荡但制度撑住"。

这场战争的走向不主要由战场决定，而由两个"外生托底"决定——俄罗斯的油价托底（受伊朗/霍尔木兹危机意外抬升）和乌克兰的外援托底（美欧政治意志）。谁的外生托底先撤，谁先接近临界。

By June 2026, the Russia-Ukraine war has solidified into a triple-attrition equilibrium: on the battlefield, drone-induced transparency has locked out maneuver for both sides; fiscally, both countries are under strain but with fundamentally different vulnerabilities — Russia is bleeding internally, chronically, under self-control; Ukraine faces an externally-imposed, potentially sudden cliff where survival depends on others.

This war's trajectory is determined not primarily by the battlefield but by two "external floors" — Russia's oil price floor (unexpectedly elevated by the Iran/Hormuz crisis) and Ukraine's external aid floor (US-European political will). Whoever's external floor collapses first approaches the critical threshold first.

SECTION 2 · 核心判断 SECTION 2 · CORE JUDGMENTS

核心判断 Core Judgments

三个关键分析判断，基于多轮研究综合，区分事实与推演。 Three key analytical judgments synthesized from multi-round research, distinguishing fact from inference.

J1

两个外生托底决定走向。战争走向由两个外生托底决定——俄罗斯油价托底（伊朗危机意外抬升：3月Brent +52%，4月Urals $112.3/桶，超G7价格上限155%）与乌克兰外援托底（美欧政治意志：欧盟€90B贷款2026-27，PURL机制）。谁的托底先撤，谁先接近临界。这不是对等的结构——俄罗斯是内生失血可自控，乌克兰是命在他人的悬崖。 Two external floors determine the outcome. The war trajectory is determined by two external floors — Russia's oil price floor (unexpectedly elevated by the Iran crisis: March Brent +52%, April Urals $112.3/bbl, 155% above G7 price cap) and Ukraine's external aid floor (US-European political will: EU €90B loan 2026–27, PURL mechanism). Whoever's floor collapses first approaches the critical threshold first. This is not a symmetric structure — Russia's bleeding is internal and self-controlled; Ukraine's cliff depends entirely on others.

J2

战场锁死在三重约束稳态。三个互锁约束同时绑死：① 兵力——俄月损耗35,203人（2026年4月），招募28,200-30,000/月，已超临界；② 装备——苏联存量装备见底，APC库存降至战前39%，IISS判断"无足够坦克支撑有效进攻"；③ 无人机透明化——FPV+光纤无人机造出15-40km击杀区，任何兵力集结在集结前即被发现摧毁。双方均无突破能力。这是"定格运动"，不是任何一方即将突破的前兆。 Battlefield locked in triple-constraint equilibrium. Three interlocking constraints simultaneously bind: ① Manpower — Russian monthly losses 35,203 (April 2026), recruitment 28,200–30,000/mo, already past critical threshold; ② Equipment — Soviet-era stockpiles exhausted, APC inventory down to 39% of pre-war levels, IISS judges insufficient tanks for effective offensives; ③ Drone transparency — FPV + fiber-optic drones create 15–40km kill zones; any force concentration is detected and destroyed before it assembles. Neither side can break through. This is "stop-motion war," not a precursor to any imminent breakthrough.

J3

中国对乌克兰无人机的近期钳制能力被高估。RUSI 2025年11月深度研究（18位专家访谈）明确判断：乌克兰的无人机生产模式近期不受中国供应链断供威胁——双重用途结构让零部件禁令结构上无法执行；乌方月产FPV拦截无人机>2,000架/天，长程FP-1无人机3,000架/月，已有工业规模对等能力。真正的命门是中国对西方主权生产的长期掌控。 China's near-term chokehold on Ukrainian drones is overstated. RUSI November 2025 deep research (18 expert interviews) explicitly concludes: Ukraine's drone production model faces no near-term threat from Chinese supply chain disruption — the dual-use structure makes component bans structurally unenforceable; Ukraine now produces >2,000 FPV intercept drones/day, 3,000 long-range FP-1 drones/month, achieving industrial-scale parity. The real long-term vulnerability is China's control over Western sovereign production.
